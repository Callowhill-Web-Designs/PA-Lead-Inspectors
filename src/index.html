Our Services

Comprehensive Lead Inspection Services

PA Lead Inspectors provides thorough lead-based paint inspections for residential and commercial properties across Pennsylvania. Our EPA-certified inspectors use state-of-the-art technology to detect lead hazards accurately and efficiently.

We serve homeowners, real estate professionals, property managers, childcare facilities, and contractors who need reliable lead testing to ensure compliance with federal and state regulations.
