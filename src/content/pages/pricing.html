Complete Service

What Every Inspection Includes

When you schedule a lead inspection with PA Lead Inspectors LLC, you're getting comprehensive service from start to finish. Our EPA-certified inspectors arrive on time with professional-grade sampling equipment and follow strict protocols established by the Environmental Protection Agency and Pennsylvania Department of Health.

The inspection process begins with a thorough visual assessment of all painted surfaces throughout your property, identifying areas of deteriorating paint, friction surfaces, and potential lead hazards. We then collect dust wipe samples from floors and window sills in each bedroom and common living area using sterile sampling materials. These samples are immediately labeled, documented, and sent to an EPA-recognized laboratory for precise lead content analysis.

Within 3-5 business days, you'll receive a comprehensive written report detailing our findings, laboratory results, comparisons to EPA safety standards, and specific recommendations for any necessary remediation or ongoing maintenance. This report is suitable for Pennsylvania rental property licensing, real estate disclosures, or your personal records. We're always available to answer questions and explain results in plain language.
