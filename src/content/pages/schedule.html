How to Prepare

  • Clear Access: Ensure our inspector can access all areas of the property including closets and basements.
  • Remove Obstacles: Move furniture or items blocking walls, windows, doors, and trim where paint testing will occur.
  • Pets: Please secure pets in a separate room or area during the inspection.
  • Utilities: Make sure electricity and water are turned on for the inspection.
  • Property Access: Provide keys, gate codes, or any necessary access information prior to the appointment.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that schedules change. If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, please provide at least 24 hours notice.

  • 24+ Hours Notice: Full refund or free rescheduling, no questions asked.
  • Less than 24 Hours: 50% cancellation fee may apply.
  • No Show: Full inspection fee will be charged.

What to Expect

  • Duration: Most inspections take 30-90 minutes depending on property size.
  • Process: Our EPA-certified inspector will test painted surfaces throughout the property.
  • Report: Receive your comprehensive inspection report within 48-72 hours.
  • Presence: You don't need to be present during the inspection, but you're welcome to be there.
  • Questions: We'll contact you immediately if we find any lead hazards or have questions.
